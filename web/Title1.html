Fashion has to reflect who you are

Posted by Leonor and Lea on 27 Jan, 2021 comments(2)

Together, fashion and personality go along. What a person looks on the outside, I think, resembles how she looks on the inside. Upon observation, a lot of speculation regarding a person is possibly based on what she wears, or how she dresses. One can look at a person's clothing much like emotions and somewhat foreshadow what that person is going to be like. Fashion, if properly read, can reveal a wide variety of thingsâ€“ inner thoughts, habits, values, personal memories, and personality traits. For example, by simply observing a specific article of clothing, such as a t-shirt, I can gain a vivid picture of the person who wears it. Fashion portrays a character, as individuals typically wear what they want and what makes them feel good about themselves. It is very difficult to keep up with each generation's trend in this day and age, whether it is how I carry myself through my clothes or how I reflect myself through my personality. Every person or group has his or her style. The styles reflect their way of living. She tends to be more casual in their choice of clothes if one plays sports. He or she appears to be meticulous in selecting clothes if one socializes with a snobbish crowd. Based on their personality, individuals have their own choice of clothing. Best of all, your look will change because your character is portrayed especially, and better yet, you did it alone.
