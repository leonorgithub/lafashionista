Fashion is a style of life

Posted by Lea Mae Sañoza on 27 Jan, 2021 comments(2)

Everyone has a unique sense of fashion. Fashion is a reflection of your way of life, if you pay attention to the little details, whether you are dressed formally or informally, whether you wear heels or no heels if you wear make-up or not if you have arranged your accessories or wear heirlooms, if you know and care about what goods and brands you buy and how you buy, What things you go to wearing the fashion of these clothes denotes character and character. Fashion will always be a product of your personality and lifestyle. Fashion and lifestyle are inseparable; whether you’re wearing activewear Fashion and lifestyle are inseparable; the things you wear will tell a lot about you, whether you wear activewear to go to the gym with friends, or whether you wear a suit to meet a friend for coffee because your lifestyle and choices are your way of giving your life meaning. For better or worse, fashion is an industry of three trillion dollars that can influence our lives. We live in a highly materialistic world, fueled by technology, convenience, and throwaway culture. We are continuously being bombarded with offers to purchase more, more, more. Many young girls are affected by the relentless flurry to buy products that prey on our insecurities, particularly when it comes to clothing and the message that consumerism is good and will bring you happiness has spread through all our media like ink in water: ads, TV product placements, newspapers, and social media.

So, fashion is a way of life the way you dress up, the way you wear your shoes, what you wear, where you buy, how you buy, where you wear your outfits and accessories, your disposable income, and your lifestyle are defined. You can afford better designs and diverse fashion if you have a better and higher lifestyle with higher income, while if you are from a lower-income community, you may not be able to afford all fashion brands, but in today's world, because of the global scope of fashion markets, you can still access affordable fashion or better still major brands' imitations and still have the satisfaction of being fashionable. Hence fashion and lifestyle are inseparable.
